# aud2sg

If you use [StoryGraph](https://app.thestorygraph.com/) to track your reading and you listen to audiobooks on Audible, you know the pain. There's no import. You have to go in and manually mark every book as read, one at a time, dig up the finish date, hope you remember it. I have years of listening history and I was never going to do that by hand.

So I built [aud2sg](https://github.com/edwinavalos/aud2sg), an MCP server that gives Claude the tools to do it for you. Point it at your Audible account and your StoryGraph account, ask Claude to do the import, and it handles the matching and marking with finish dates pulled straight from Audible.

It's rough around the edges but it solved my problem. Check it out if you're in the same situation.
